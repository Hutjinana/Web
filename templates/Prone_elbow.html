Prone on your elbows

How to do

  • Lie down on your stomach. Prop yourself up on your forearms with your shoulders above your elbows.
  • Hold for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Lower your upper body. Repeat up to eight times a day.
  • PROB = correctness(if this value close 1 that mean you do corrct)
  • CLASS = name of physical therapy
  • TIME = timer
  • FPS = Frame per second
*Be careful : if you do and feel pain, stop doing it immediately
